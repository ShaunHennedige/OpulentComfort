Neighborhoods

Nestled in a prime neighborhood, The Opulent Comfort - Cinnamon Life Suites places you in close proximity to iconic tourist destinations. The renowned One Galle Face shopping mall, Colombo City Centre shopping complex, Colombo Port City, 5-star hotels, and the awe-inspiring Gangaramaya Buddhist Temple are just moments away. Convenient travel is assured, with the Colombo Fort station a mere 8-minute drive and the Bandaranayake International Airport in Katunayake reachable within an hour. Culinary delights surround you, with options like Navaratna for Indian cuisine and Golden Dragon for Chinese dishes at Taj Samudra, along with the diverse, multi-country, multi-cuisine experience at Plates in Cinnamon Grand, promising a global culinary journey just steps from your door.
